✦ Quick Answers

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Fast answers to the most common StreamVerse queries — no waiting required.

How many devices can I use with my StreamVerse account?
+
Simultaneous streams depend on your plan — Mobile allows 1, Standard allows 2, and Premium allows 4 screens at once. You can install the app on unlimited devices, but only the plan-permitted number can stream simultaneously.
Can I share my StreamVerse account with family members?
+
Yes! Standard plans support up to 3 profiles and Premium supports up to 5, including a dedicated Kids profile with parental controls. Each profile gets its own personalised watchlist and recommendations. Account sharing outside your household may be restricted per our Terms of Use.
How do I cancel my StreamVerse subscription?
+
You can cancel anytime from Account → Subscription → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain full access until the end of your current billing cycle. No partial refunds are issued for the remaining period, and cancellations take effect immediately.
Is StreamVerse available on Smart TVs?
+
StreamVerse is available on Samsung (Tizen OS), LG (webOS), Sony (Android TV), and most major Smart TVs. You can also use Amazon Fire Stick, Chromecast, Apple TV, and Roku. Simply search "StreamVerse" in your device's app store and install for free.
Why is my video buffering or playing in low quality?
+
Buffering is usually caused by a slow internet connection. We recommend at least 5 Mbps for HD and 25 Mbps for 4K. Try restarting your router, switching from Wi-Fi to a wired connection, lowering quality in Settings, or closing other bandwidth-heavy apps.
How do I redeem a StreamVerse promo code?
+
Sign in and go to Account → Offers & Promos → Redeem Code. Enter the code exactly as shown — codes are case-sensitive. Valid codes are applied immediately to your account. Each promo code can only be redeemed once per account.
Can I watch StreamVerse content offline?
+
Yes! Tap the download icon on any eligible title and watch it offline in the Downloads section of the app. Downloaded content expires after a set period. The number of downloadable titles depends on your plan. Note: not all content is available for download due to licensing restrictions.
